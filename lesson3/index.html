Name

The name of the weather website is WeatherBee.

Purpose

The purpose of WeatherBee is to provide a child-friendly weather website. Most weather websites are full of a lot of content and a lot of data. This can make it hard for little children to parse. WeatherBee is focused on keeping things simple and visually fun for young children.

The second purpose is to provide educational content for children who want to learn more about the weather.
